I'm currently a junior at Lake Forest College, Illinois, studying art history and museum studies. My dream is to work in exhibition and collections management. I believe that museums are really interesting spaces that can bring people together, and that they can always strive to be better.

I've traveled a lot in life. I was born in Aarhus, Denmark (and no, I don't have Danish citizenship). As a toddler, I spent a few years in O'ahu, Hawaii, before my family moved to Madison, Wiscosin, where I grew up. A few years ago, we moved to Orem, Utah, until I came to Chicago for college. Throughout these moves, I also spent a lot of time traveling with my family, sometimes living abroad for extended periods of time in England and Germany.

As a result of this upbringing, I love traveling and getting to experience new places and cultures. I've been to over 20 countries, gone on study abroad in Berlin, and have been to more museums than I can count.

I have three adorable cats back home in Utah, where my family lives. I love watching obscure movies and reading fantasy novels. When I have time, I draw, embroider, and knit.
