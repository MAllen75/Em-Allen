I'm Em Allen, a junior studying art history and museum studies at Lake Forest College, Illinois. I'm passionate about exhibition and collections management and plan on pursuing a career in museum work.


I have always loved visiting museums and have discovered recently that I love working in them too. Some my favorite museums are the ones that are adapting and pushing the boundaries of what it means to be a museum.
